Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Haq Collectibles LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Transaction details and purchase history
  • Communications you send to us
  • Photos and descriptions of items you submit for evaluation
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process transactions and provide our services
  • Communicate with you about orders, inquiries, and services
  • Send updates about items that may interest you (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who assist our operations (payment processors, shipping carriers) under confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at info@haqcollectibles.com.

Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping (United States)

We ship via USPS, UPS, and FedEx depending on item size, value, and destination. Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days. Expedited options are available.

  • Items under $100: USPS First Class or Priority Mail
  • Items $100-$500: USPS Priority Mail with tracking and insurance
  • Items over $500: USPS Registered Mail, UPS, or FedEx with signature required

International Shipping

We ship internationally to most countries. International buyers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and import fees. Shipping times vary by destination (typically 7-21 business days). Please inquire for international shipping quotes.

Insurance

All shipments are insured for their full value. We carefully package items to prevent damage in transit. If an item arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on weight, dimensions, value, and destination. We provide shipping quotes before finalizing transactions. Free shipping may be offered on orders over certain thresholds.

Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If an item is not as described, you may return it within 14 days of receipt for a full refund of the purchase price.

Conditions for Returns

  • Item must be returned in the same condition as received
  • Original packaging and any certificates must be included
  • Contact us before returning to receive return authorization
  • Return shipping is the buyer's responsibility unless the item was misrepresented

Items Not Eligible for Return

  • Items altered, cleaned, or damaged after receipt
  • Items removed from certified holders (slabs)
  • Items returned after 14 days without prior approval
  • Items accurately described where buyer simply changed their mind

Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 5 business days of receiving the returned item and verifying its condition. Refunds are issued via the original payment method.

Disputes

If you have concerns about an item, please contact us before initiating a dispute with your payment provider. We're committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly.

Payment Methods

Accepted Payment Methods

  • PayPal — Secure online payments
  • Bank Wire/Transfer — For larger transactions
  • Personal Check — Items ship after check clears (7-10 days)
  • Money Order/Cashier's Check — Treated as cash upon verification
  • Cash — For local, in-person transactions only

Payment for Items We Purchase

When we buy items from you, payment is typically issued within 1-3 business days of receiving and verifying the items. Payment methods include:

  • Company check
  • PayPal
  • Bank wire (for amounts over $5,000)
  • Cash (for local transactions under $10,000)

Security

We never ask for credit card numbers via email. All payment information is handled through secure, encrypted channels. Be cautious of any communication asking for sensitive payment information outside of secure systems.
