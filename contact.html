For general inquiries, use this form:

Ready to sell or consign? Use this form to submit your items for evaluation. Include clear photos and as much information as you have. We'll get back to you with a preliminary assessment.

Tips for Better Evaluations:

  • Include clear, well-lit photos of the front and back
  • Photograph any damage, wear, or notable features
  • Include a size reference (coin next to a ruler, etc.)
  • Tell us anything you know about where the item came from
  • Don't clean or alter items before evaluation

Looking for something specific? Tell us what you're searching for and we'll tap our network to find it. The more details you provide, the better we can match your needs.

What to Include in Your Request:

  • Be as specific as possible (dates, mintmarks, editions, etc.)
  • Specify minimum acceptable condition
  • Let us know your budget range
  • Tell us if you need graded/certified items only
  • Mention any items you're NOT interested in (variants, etc.)

Schedule an Appointment

For large collections or items that need in-person evaluation, we're happy to schedule a meeting. Contact us via email or phone to arrange a time.
