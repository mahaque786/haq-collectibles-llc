About Haq Collectibles

Built on expertise, integrity, and a genuine passion for collectibles.

Our Story

Haq Collectibles LLC was founded with a simple mission: to bring honesty, expertise, and fair dealing to the collectibles market. What began as a personal passion for numismatics has grown into a full-service collectibles business serving collectors, estates, and institutions.

We believe that every collectible has a story, and every transaction should be built on trust. Whether you're a seasoned collector looking to add to your holdings or someone who just discovered a box of old coins in the attic, we approach every interaction with the same level of care and professionalism.

Our Philosophy

The collectibles market can be intimidating. Values fluctuate, authenticity is paramount, and finding trustworthy partners isn't always easy. We've built our business on being the kind of dealer we'd want to work with ourselves:

  • Transparent pricing — We explain how we arrive at our valuations
  • Honest assessments — We'll tell you what you have, even if it's not what you hoped
  • No pressure — Take your time; we're here when you're ready
  • Education-focused — We want you to understand the market, not just trust us blindly

Areas of Expertise

Our knowledge spans multiple collecting categories, developed through years of hands-on experience, continuous research, and active participation in the collector community. Our primary areas of expertise include:

  • U.S. and World Coins (including ancient coinage)
  • Paper Currency and Banknotes
  • Rare and Antiquarian Books
  • Trading Cards (sports, TCG, vintage)
  • Vintage Video Games and Retro Technology
  • Historical Ephemera and Documents
  • Exonumia (tokens, medals, and related items)

Our Vetting Process

Every item we handle goes through a careful evaluation process. We examine physical characteristics, research provenance when available, consult reference materials, and when appropriate, utilize third-party authentication services. We maintain an extensive reference library and stay current with market trends and authentication techniques.

For high-value items, we can facilitate grading through recognized services such as NGC, PCGS, PSA, CGC, and others. We believe in full disclosure about condition issues and will never misrepresent what we're selling.

Ethics & Transparency

We operate with complete transparency in all our dealings. This means:

  • Clear explanations of how we determine value
  • Honest condition assessments with no hidden flaws
  • Written documentation for all significant transactions
  • Respect for provenance and legal ownership
  • Compliance with all applicable laws and regulations

We do not knowingly deal in stolen property, items of questionable provenance, or anything that violates cultural heritage laws. If we can't verify an item's legitimacy, we won't handle it.
